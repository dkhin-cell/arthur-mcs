# Arthur MCS — Decision Log

This directory contains the decision records for every major product, architecture, and strategy decision made during the development of Arthur · Mission Control System.

## The Director Model

Arthur MCS is built by DK (David Aung Khin) as sole founder, product leader, and Director of a multi-agent AI system. DK sets the vision, identifies the problems, makes the judgment calls, and directs four specialized AI agents:

- **Claude** — CTO Advisor (system architecture, technical implementation, forward-looking infrastructure)
- **Carter** — Research/Data Agent (structured JSON, Notion roadmap, quality standards)
- **Daniel** — COO Advisor (operational strategy, competitive analysis, commercial positioning)
- **Gemini** — External Analysis (market research, PM critique)

Every decision flows through DK. The agents propose, debate, and build — but DK directs. These logs capture that process: DK's strategic vision, the agent debates it triggered, the judgment calls DK made, and what shipped as a result.

## How to Read These

Each file follows a consistent structure. The most important sections are:

- **DK's Strategic Vision / Original Idea** — what DK identified as the problem or opportunity
- **The Debate** — where agents pushed back and DK evaluated their input
- **The Decision** — what DK chose, what DK rejected, and the tradeoff DK accepted

The logs demonstrate product leadership, technical decision-making, and multi-agent AI orchestration — all directed by a single founder operating at the Principal PM to VP level.
